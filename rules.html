📜 Server Rules

🚫 General Rules

  • No hacking, cheating, or using mods that give unfair advantage
  • No exploiting bugs or duplication glitches
  • No spam or advertising other servers
  • Respect all players and staff
  • No racist, toxic, or inappropriate behavior

⚔️ PvP Rules

  • No combat logging
  • No safezone abuse during combat
  • No teaming in unfair ways (if disallowed by server mode)

🏠 Base & Griefing Rules

  • Griefing is allowed outside protected areas (if SMP rules allow)
  • No stealing in safe regions
  • No abusing claims or protections

🔧 Staff Rules

  • Do not impersonate staff
  • Staff decisions are final
  • Report bugs instead of exploiting them

⚠️ Punishments

  • Breaking rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans
  • Severe exploits = permanent ban
